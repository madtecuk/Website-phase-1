ABOUT

M.A.D. tec UK is a conveniently packaged IT department that offers a custom service for small and medium businesses. IT Support, Application Development, Web Development, Hardware/Software Supply & IT Project Management are a sample of the services we provide.

As developers of varying technical backgrounds, we understand the many needs within a business and the demand for a swift delivery of projects and services. M.A.D. tec UK employ a culture that emphasizes the importance of quality of service, customer relations and transparency of process. We feel these values are important and must always take precedent over profit.

We are dedicated to implementing a wide range of effective and long-lasting solutions to your business, to provide you with confidence and piece of mind in your IT Infrastructure, allowing you to concentrate on what you do best.


VALUES


MISSION: We, here at M.A.D. tec UK, are commited to providing a service that is custom tailored to the needs of our customers. We strive for our passion to be reflected within every project we put our name to, be it desktop support to managing IT departments. VISION: We believe that M.A.D. tec UK will become a brand trusted by small and medium businesses for its competent and cost effective IT Services. The pride we take in our work will easily distinguish us as dependable and professional, as we feel that this is how a company should be.
